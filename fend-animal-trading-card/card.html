Shar-pei

It is believed that the Shar-Pei is an ancient breed dating back to the Han Dynasty (200 B.C.) in the southern provinces of China due to statues found in tombs resembling the wrinkled pups....

  • Scientific name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Avarage Lifespan: 9 to 11 years
  • Height: 46 to 56 cm
  • Color:coat red, red fawn, , black, black silver sables, cream...

The Chinese Shar-Pei is a large dog with wrinkled skin. It has a square profile with a broad, flat head. The muzzle is wide, padded and full, with a moderate stop. Like the Chow Chow, these dogs have a blue-black tongue. The teeth meet in a scissors bite. The small, sunken, almond-shaped eyes are dark, but may be lighter in dogs with a dilute colored coat. The high-set, triangular ears are very small and slightly rounded at the tips.
